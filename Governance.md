# LXVII Governance Framework

## Overview

LXVII is transitioning into a governance-based ecosystem where major decisions are made by token holders.

Governance will be implemented through a Solana DAO structure.

---

## Governance Principles

- 1 LXVII = 1 Vote
- No APY or financial yield model
- Community participation encouraged
- Transparent treasury usage

---

## Proposal Process

Proposals may include:

- Token burn mechanisms
- Treasury allocation
- NFT initiatives
- Launchpad Light approvals
- Ecosystem development decisions

Minimum token threshold for proposal submission may apply.

Voting duration: Approx. 3 days  
Quorum requirements: To be defined based on circulating supply.
